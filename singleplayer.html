Minecraft
Minecraft is a well-known sandbox video game created by Markus Persson, popularly known as "Notch," and published by Mojang Studios. It was initially launched as a PC game in 2009 and has subsequently been converted to a variety of platforms, including consoles and mobile devices.

Minecraft has received several upgrades throughout the years, providing new features and gameplay concepts. It has spawned a large community of gamers, content providers, and modders, and has become a cultural phenomenon. Minecraft has also been utilized as an instructional tool as well as a platform for a variety of events such as concerts and festivals. MInecraft has been a staple in my childhood.
